About Us

AetherAI is an orginisation of future researchers and developers focused on AI development, scaling, and open source AI research. Founded in Febuary of 2022, we are primarily focused on building and OpenSourcing large ML models and datasets ranging from image generation like VQGAN+CLIP to large language models like JAX or GPT. If your looking to join the team, our Discord server is open and welcomes contributors!
